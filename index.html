About Me

Sarah DiCalogero

ABOUT

Sarah is currently an Assistant Professor of Mathematics at Tidewater Community College in Norfolk, VA. She holds a Master’s in Statistics and a Baccalaureate degree in Applied Mathematics and Economics from the University of Virginia. She has also completed graduate coursework at Old Dominion University in Modeling and Simulation, Statistics and Instructional Design.

She currently teaches online asynchronous and synchronous courses in Math and Statistics at Tidewater Community College. She has used OER (Open Educational Resources) extensively during her 15 years at the college to develop text-book fee free courses for her students.

Originally from Northern Virginia, Sarah has lived in the Hampton Roads area for the past 24 years and considers Norfolk her “hometown” where she lives with her husband, three daughters and their Covid puppy, Roxy. In her free time she to spend time with family and friends, volunteer and drink wine.
